Business phone and messaging system for teams. Rippledesk allows you to make and receive calls from your WordPress site.

== Description ==
Great for SMS marketing, phone and website calls. Rippledesk provides merchants with voice and SMS solutions. Phone numbers have both calls and SMS capabilities. The website widget enables you to receive internet calls. Setup welcome messages, business hours, record calls, voicemail, and IVR. Using campaigns, you can send out bulk SMS and Voice messages to your customers.

A dedicated business phone number for SMS, making and receiving calls
Receive website calls through a floating widget
Unlimited Calls and SMS
Send Voice and SMS campaigns
Phone system with IVR, auto-recording, call summary, business hours, and more
